Pasta with Pesto

With Italy as my neighbor country, pasta and pesto are commonplace in supermarkets and a staple for students or families.

Ingredients (per adult)

  • 250g pasta
  • 50g pesto
  • 50g parmesan (ideally not grated beforehand)
  • water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Put water in a pot (enough to cover the pasta when it is softened) and bring it to a boil
  2. Salt heavily, then add the pasta. Give it a stir. You can now turn down the heat a little bit, it should simmer but it doesn't have to boil. Cover half of the pot with a lid to preserve the heat
  3. Wait for the time given on the pasta packaging. Then try some noodles. You want them to still have a bit of bite, so ideally you should stop heating them as they are a bit too tough, because they will continue to cook a bit afterwards
  4. Drain the pasta, mix it with the pesto, grate and add parmesan as you like

Enjoy!
